A introduction into Seed Bomb making
  1. Mix together 1 cup of seeds with 5 cups of compost and 2-3 cups of clay powder
  2. Then slowly mix in water with your hands until everything sticks
  3. Roll mixture into firm balls
  4. Leave the balls to dry in a sunny spot
  5. Plant your seed bombs by throwing them at bare parts of the garden and wait to see what pops up
